About Us

The Kookie Company, was founded by Daphne Magsby after she discovered her great-grand mother's handwritten cookie recipes while helping her mother sort through boxes that were likely older than her. After spending the following weekend trying out the different recipes, there was no doubt these cookies were something special.



Upon letting family and friends try the cookies and hearing the rave reviews and requests that she make more for every occassion, Daphne knew she had to honor her great-grand mother's legacy by creating The Kookie Company and allowing everyone a taste cookie deliciousness.
